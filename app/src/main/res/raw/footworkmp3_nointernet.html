Sound track for Footwork training

Audio leader can be used by individual fencers or by groups; for warm-up, footwork training, or special fitness training. The composition of the footwork training exercises are based on the Tyshler Footwork Training DVD and is suitable for beginners and top class fencers.
The 10 min workout starts from en garde position and steps, followed by two and three stages of attack combinations, combinations of manoeuvring, and most commonly used by fencers during bouts - specially selected by Dr David and Dr Gennady Tyshler. The sound track includes background music and tuition, which will lead you through the exercises, making your training easier and more enjoyable.
